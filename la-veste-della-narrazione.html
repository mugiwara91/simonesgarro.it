La veste
della narrazione

"La veste della narrazione" that literally means "The dress of the story" is my personal dissertation in design school. It explores the importance of book covers, book design and how they can influence our choice.

It search otherwise the history of book covers and who are by now the more influencing Illustrators and book designers.
