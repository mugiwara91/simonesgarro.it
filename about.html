Who I am

I'm Simone Sgarro and I'm an Italian graphic designer based in Turin. I enjoy creating visual and more over brand identity to put a face on agencies. I try to do this using my skills in logo design, brand identity, brand design, promotion and everything they need.

I am always inspired by masters such as Massimo Vignelli, Bob Noorda but also contemporary ones with different styles like Paula Sher, Michael Bierut and Aaron Draplin.

When I'm not designing I love shoting with my camera, cook, and play music with guitar, clarinet, piano and other instruments.
